Did You Know?

Gandhari’s curse was the reason for Lord Krishna’s death.

Eklavya, who gave his thumb as gurudakshina to Drona, was later killed by Lord Krishna. He was reborn as Drishtadyumna and ultimately took vengeance on Drona.

Lord Krishna was related to the Pandavas through their mother’s side. Kunti, the mother of the Pandavas, was the sister of Vasudev, Lord Krishna’s father.

Arjun cremated all the Yadavs, including Vasudev.

The name Krishna has a profound meaning. It is derived from the Sanskrit words 'Krish,' meaning 'to attract,' and 'na,' meaning 'supreme joy.' Thus, Krishna means 'one who attracts all with supreme joy.'
